ZENTOOL DUAL ACTION POLISHER

ZENTOOL Dual Action Polisher represents one of the latest advancements in machine polishing technology. With an orbit (throw) of 12 and 21mm, the dual action polisher has evolved to become a leader in efficient cutting power while still maintaining the safe operation expected from a DA polisher. Compared to rotary polisher this machine achieves a far superior finish with no holograms.

Due to the large orbit diameter, buffing pad weight plays a major role in maintaining a smooth operating machine. Ideal pad weight for the ZENTOOL is up to 27g and comes with free 5″ and 6″ backing plates and a 1-year warranty.

ZEN-12EM2

ZEN-12EM2 mini dual action has a 75mm(3”)backing plate to operate smaller pads and allow fast correction of tight spaces where large machines cannot operate.

  • Highly Efficient 380w motor
  • 12 mm orbit
  • 76mm backing plate
ZEN-21E2
